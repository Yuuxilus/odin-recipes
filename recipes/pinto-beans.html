Pressure Cooker Pinto Beans Recipe

This post shows how to cook pinto beans in a pressure cooker. In 1 hour, you can have rich, tender, creamy, ready-to-enjoy beans all soupy in a nutritious cooking broth!

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¼ cups of pound dried pinto beans
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 3/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 minced clove garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric,salt,pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato paste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse and pick over the beans, looking for little stones that might be masquerading as beans.
  2. Put the rinsed beans into the pressure cooker. Add the olive oil to prevent foaming. Add the onion and the garlic. Cover with hot water—3 inches above the level of the beans.
  3. Add the tomato paste and season the beans with the spices.
  4. Bring the pot to pressure and cook for the allotted time
  5. Test a bean to make sure it is tender to your liking. If the beans need to cook more, bring to pressure again for a minute or so.
  6. You are there—perfectly cooked pinto beans!
